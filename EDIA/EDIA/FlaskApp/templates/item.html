Welcome




Autism is a neurological disorder that causes communication difficulties and difficulties socialize with others. Autistic children usually speak little, ignore others and come back again and again on the actions and behaviors such as spin in circles or focus on a particular object of hours. They excel in activities that require attention to the details, such as spelling or instruments but are upset when they are faced with a whole world of life
